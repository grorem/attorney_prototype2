Contracts

If you are an employee, then you have rights that are protected under State and federal laws. Attorney Rorem has defended clients in many employment law cases including:

  • - Discrimination
  • - Wrongful termination
  • - Wage and overtime claims
  • - Breach of contract


Case highlight: In a recent case, Attorney Rorem represented an employee who alleged a major corporation had terminated him for reasons of age and religion. Although the corporation denied it, he successfully helped his client reach an agreeable settlement.
