Accidents & Injuries

Attorney Rorem has represented parties in:

  • - Automobile accidents
  • - Slip and fall
  • - Wage and overtime claims
  • - Breach of contract
  • - Medical device failure litigations


Case highlight: in one memorable case, a retailer had a problem with the automatic doors that opened as a customer approached the business. Unbeknownst to the retailer, the company that serviced the door disabled the mechanism that kept the door open while a person passed through the doorway. As a result, the door only stayed open a few seconds, which would be enough for most people to pass through the doorway. An elderly woman who moved slowly, using a walker, was knocked to the ground and injured when the door closed on her since the electric sensor that should have kept the door from closing had been disabled. The case was settled to everyone’s satisfaction
