THE PROCESS

A good attorney has a clear process to help his/her clients win a lawsuit. The below section explains the simple five-step consultation process with me.

1

I will review your case with you and tell you what I can do for you. I will provide the reasons if I do not believe I can help you. I’ll then discuss possibilities with you and suggest a further course of action.

2

Once I fully understand your issue or desired goal, I will find relevent aspects of the law to apply to your case and to meet its purpose.

3

We will disccuss the possible actions we can take - the process which must be followed to meet your goal, as well as the estimated time and expense to complete the case.

4

After discussing everything together, we will agree on the course of action to take.

5

I will have regular consultations with you to maintain a constant communication to improve the success of the case.
